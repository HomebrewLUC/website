Who are we?

homebrew@LUC is an organization revolving around anything and everything technology encompasses, with a passion to learn and teach about the subject. We believe that as people transition into the 21st century, that knowing some amount of programming is absolutley essential. As such, our members come from all different majors and minors, and encompass a wide variety of interests. Some of us are scientists, others are businessmen and businessowoman, others are hackers, developers, photogoraphers, musicians, etc. We are all passionate about technology, and can clearly see the effects of it today. As such, we wanted to create a community of students at Loyola University Chicago, and provide them with the ability to pursue their own ideas, and foster an enviorment where everyone can learn. Knowledge is power. This club is the realization of that dream.
